Famous Planetariums in India

Explore the cosmos through these iconic destinations.

Nehru Planetarium, New Delhi

A hub for astronomy enthusiasts dedicated to Jawaharlal Nehru.

Key Features:
  • State-of-the-art projection system
  • Interactive space exhibits
  • Regular astronomy shows
Timings:

10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)

Birla Planetarium, Kolkata

One of the largest planetariums in Asia, known as the "Taramandal."

Key Features:
  • Immersive 360-degree dome shows
  • Historic astronomy artifacts
  • Educational programs for students
Timings:

11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Jawaharlal Nehru Planetarium, Bengaluru

A destination for science enthusiasts offering engaging space shows.

Key Features:
  • Interactive science park
  • Monthly skywatching events
  • Cutting-edge projection systems
Timings:

10:30 AM - 5:30 PM (Closed on Mondays)

Priyadarshini Planetarium, Thiruvananthapuram

An advanced planetarium showcasing the wonders of the universe.

Key Features:
  • High-definition video projections
  • Space-themed exhibits
  • Modern educational facilities
Timings:

10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)
